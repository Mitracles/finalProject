About

South Korean Music Academt established in 2005 by Bang Si-hyuk as Big Hit Entertainment Academy. This elite institution operates as a record label, talent agency, music production company, school management and learning facility, and as a music publishing house. The company has multiple subsidiaries, including Big Hit Music, Source Music, Pledis Entertainment, Belift Lab, and KOZ Entertainment, collectively known as Hybe Academy.
